Revocatus Installer

The easiest way to clean up your interaction history. No apps or setups required.

Long-Press to Bookmark Revocatus

How to use on Mobile Chrome:

  1. Long-press the blue button above and tap "Copy link address".
  2. Tap the browser's 3-dot menu and hit the Star (⭐) icon to bookmark this page.
  3. Tap Edit at the bottom of your screen, name it Revocatus, clear the URL box, and paste what you copied.
  4. Now, go to Facebook Activity Log -> Likes and Reactions, open your Bookmarks, and tap Revocatus!
