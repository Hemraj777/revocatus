Revocatus Installer

The easiest way to clean up your interaction history. No apps or setups required.

Long-Press to Bookmark Revocatus

How to use on Mobile:

  1. Long-press the blue button above and select "Bookmark link".
  2. Open Facebook in this browser and go to your Activity Log -> Likes and Reactions.
  3. Open your browser settings menu, go to Bookmarks, and tap Revocatus to start cleaning!
